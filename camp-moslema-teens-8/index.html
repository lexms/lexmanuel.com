WHAT IS CMTEENS?
CMTeens is an annual event held by Nurul Fikri Boarding School’s student organization, is it intended to prepare the next generation for the future of humanity in Southeast Asia especially in Indonesia. We hold CMTeens event aimed at building the spirit of young people especially students. The aim is to make this generation more loving the homeland and spread the spirit of unity, especially in terms of uniting differences between tribes, religion, develop creativity, and self-development. In addition, this event is also intended as a means of adhesive of solidarity between Southeast Asian students and self actualization media.
